Become a Partner

Partnership Levels:

Individual Partners

  • $30 annual dues with a sliding scale option
  • $15 for students
  • Dues can be waived for in-kind donations to GPSEN

Sustaining Partners

  • Provide significant contributions of financial support
    • Minimum of $5,000 annual contribution
    • Three tiers:
    • $5,000 - $9,999 (Bronze)
    • $10,000 - $14,999 (Silver)
    • $15,000 and above (Gold)

Contributing Partners

  • Dues vary according to organization sector & revenue with a sliding scale ($50 to $5,000)
    • Private
      • $250 per $1 million in annual revenues with a max of $5,000
    • Nonprofit
      • $50, minimum. $100 per $1 million in annual revenues with a max of $5,000
    • Public Agency
      • $100 - $1,000 for small agencies (<$150 million budget)
      • $1,000 - $3,000 for medium agencies ($150-250 million budget)
      • $3,000 - $5,000 for large agencies (>$250 million budget)
  • Partner Exchange Option available at the Contributing Partner level
  • Dues can be waived for in-kind donations to GPSEN

Partner Benefits:

GPSEN provides a vibrant network of outreach tools, training opportunities, partner support, curriculum, research, and resources that help build capacity.

Member benefits for all levels include:

  • Invited to participate in Partner Meetings
  • Discounted rates to attend GPSEN events
  • Opportunities to serve on working groups and committees
  • Networking opportunities through GPSEN and partner connections
  • Recognition as a partner on GPSEN’s website (optional for individuals)
  • Training/Professional Development opportunities

Benefits for Sustaining & Contributing Partners:

  • Promote events and resources via GPSEN newsletter, calendar, website, and social media
  • Coverage of news in GPSEN newsletter
  • Share stories and updates on GPSEN social media platforms (including Facebook, Twitter, etc.)
  • Recognition as a partner on GPSEN website
  • Tabling and sharing opportunities at GPSEN-hosted events
  • Opportunities for recognition and participation at regional, national and international conferences
  • Collaborate on organizational Initiatives
  • Partner Support for capacity-building
  • Opportunities to develop and recruit student internships
  • Collaborate on research and international work with GPSEN and other partner organizations
  • Support for projects through GPSEN’s partnerships, linking resources with needs
  • Collaborate on grants and/or receive letters of support for partner projects & initiatives
  • Opportunities to co-sponsor GPSEN events

Additional benefits for Sustaining Partners:

  • Listed as a Sustaining Partner on the partners page and logo displayed on the GPSEN website footer
  • Priority location and free tabling at events
  • Additional opportunities for featured news in GPSEN newsletter and on website
